Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Days in Office ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data: The app uses your device's GPS to detect when you enter and exit designated office locations. This location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve app performance. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

How We Use Your Information

Your location data is used solely to determine your presence at configured office locations and calculate your attendance. We do not:

  • Upload your location data to any server
  • Share your location data with third parties
  • Track your location outside of configured office geofences
  • Sell or monetize your personal data in any way

Data Storage

All attendance records and location data are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently removed. We do not maintain backups of your personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple's StoreKit for subscription management. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Export your attendance data from within the app

Children's Privacy

Days in Office is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
