No. Days at the Office uses iOS's built-in geofencing technology, which is extremely battery-efficient. The app only activates when you cross the boundary of a location you've set up—it doesn't constantly track your GPS position in the background. Most users report no noticeable impact on battery life.
When you add a location, you set a detection radius (the default is 100 meters). The app uses GPS geofencing to detect when you enter and exit that area. It then calculates how long you were there and determines whether it counts as a "day" based on your configured time threshold (for example, 2 hours minimum).
Free users can track one location with full functionality. Pro unlocks up to 20 active locations—perfect for people who split time between multiple offices, client sites, or co-working spaces. Pro also includes PDF and CSV exports for your personal records. Pro is $39.99/year or $4.99/month.
A qualified day is any calendar day where your total time at a location meets or exceeds your configured threshold. The default is 2 hours, but you can adjust this in each location's settings. If you have multiple short visits on the same day, the app adds them together—so a morning session plus an afternoon session can still count as one qualified day.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

Best Day Labs LLC ("we," "our," or "the app") operates the Days at the Office mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Our Privacy Commitment: Your location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We are committed to protecting your privacy and comply with applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data (Processed Locally Only)

The app uses your device's GPS to detect when you enter and exit designated locations you configure. This location data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device using iOS geofencing capabilities
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Stored only in the app's local database on your device
  • Used solely to calculate your attendance
  • Permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Usage Data (Anonymous and Aggregated)

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics through Apple's App Analytics to improve app performance and user experience. This data:

  • Cannot be used to identify individual users
  • Includes information such as app crashes, feature usage, and device types
  • Is collected only if you have enabled "Share Analytics" in your iOS settings
  • Does not include any location information

Account and Subscription Data

If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes all payment information. We receive only:

  • Subscription status (active/expired)
  • Subscription tier (monthly/annual)
  • Anonymous transaction identifiers for verification

We do not receive or store your payment card information, billing address, or full name from Apple.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when granting location permissions and agreeing to these terms
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to provide the attendance tracking service you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: Anonymous analytics help us improve app stability and features while not overriding your privacy rights

3. How We Use Your Information

Your locally-stored location data is used solely to:

  • Detect your presence at locations you configure
  • Calculate your attendance hours and qualified days
  • Generate attendance reports and analytics within the app
  • Export attendance data at your request

We explicitly do NOT:

  • Upload your location data to any server or cloud service
  • Share your location data with third parties
  • Track your location outside of configured geofences
  • Sell, rent, or monetize your personal data in any way
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Monitor your location when the app is uninstalled or location permissions are revoked

4. Data Storage and Retention

All attendance records and location data are stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. Data is retained:

  • Until you manually delete it within the app
  • Until you uninstall the app (all data is permanently removed)
  • As long as you keep the app installed and wish to maintain your records

We do not maintain server-side backups of your personal data.

5. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management and in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • iOS Location Services: For geofencing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

We do not integrate analytics services, advertising networks, or other third-party trackers beyond Apple's native services.

6. Your Privacy Rights

For All Users:

  • Access: View all your attendance data within the app at any time
  • Export: Export your attendance data in CSV format
  • Delete: Delete specific attendance records or all data within the app
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable location tracking at any time in iOS Settings > Days at the Office > Location
  • Complete Removal: Uninstall the app to permanently delete all associated data

For EEA/UK/Swiss Users (GDPR Rights):

In addition to the above, you have the right to:

  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data (you can edit records in the app)
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format (CSV export)
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

For California Users (CCPA Rights):

California residents have the right to:

  • Know: Request disclosure of what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information, so opt-out is not applicable
  • Non-Discrimination: Exercise your privacy rights without discriminatory treatment

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at bestdaylabs@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days (45 days for CCPA requests).

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Days at the Office is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at bestdaylabs@gmail.com, and we will assist in deleting such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Since all location data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, there are no international data transfers of your location information. Anonymous analytics data may be transmitted to Apple servers located in the United States and other countries where Apple operates, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and data protection commitments.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Local data storage is protected by iOS's encryption and security features
  • The app does not transmit location data over networks, eliminating interception risks
  • Access to locally stored data requires device authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode)
